## Lucene parser caveat and raw test-output cross-check
The Lucene `resolved=false` result is a known Gradle parser false negative. The parser loses the association between a test and its `PASSED` line when the JVM emits the Security Manager deprecation warning between the test line and its result. The raw output is therefore authoritative for this transport/oracle audit, while `report.json` remains the harness score artifact.
